Where It Started

Is there a way to predict the outcome of baseball games based on historical data and betting odds? That's what we wanted to know! Can we analyze historical game data and make accurate predictions as to who you should bet on to win? We wanted to look at different aspects of the stats to determine what route would be consistent with an overall profit throughout a baseball season. If you had just one dollar to bet on each game of the season, who should you put your money on? All home games? All away games? The underdogs? And what's the breaking point for the Line? Should I bet if it's under 150? Over 150? Another number?

Well sit back and relax because we've done all the hard work for you! A quick walkthrough of our site will give you all the information you need to satisfy all your questions!
